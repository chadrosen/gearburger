Frequently Asked Questions

What is Gear Burger?
Gear Burger is a free service committed to delivering you the gear deals you're looking for.
How do I get started and create some alerts?
From Home, click "Start Now". First, tell us what types of gear and brands you'd like to hear about. When you click submit we'll send you an email that will contain a link that you'll need to click to activate your account and alerts. You should change your password once you can access your account to something you can remember.
How do I log in?
Click Login in the upper navigation bar and then enter the email address and password on your account.
How do I resend my password?
If you can't remember your password, click I forgot my password and follow the prompts to reset your password. If know your password but would like to change it, login to your account and click the "Change Password" link within your home page and follow the prompts you reset your password.
Why am I not getting my Gear Burger emails?
Check your spam filter! Be sure to add to your address book or white list. Also, if your alert parameters are very specific, it may be a long time before we see a deal that is a match. Try adding more brands and/or more categories.
What sites do you monitor?
We currently monitor backcountry.com, DogFunk.com, hucknroll.com, RealCyclist.com with more on the way!
How can I get Gear Burger to monitor deals on a site that I like?
We love feedback and suggestions! Submit a message in our support forum and we'll be sure to check it out!
What if my question wasn't answered here?
We try to keep this FAQ page as helpful and current as possible, but if you have a question that needs answering or if you'd simply like to give us a shout out, visit our support forum. If you've had a problem, chances are someone else has or will too.
